# Reading Response Assignment 1

## Due Date
Week 3, Sunday 11:59 PM

## Prompt
Compare and contrast the conceptions of popular sovereignty in John Locke’s *Second Treatise* and Jean‑Jacques Rousseau’s *Social Contract*. In your response (500‑750 words), address the following:
- How does each author define the source of political authority?
- What role do consent and the general will play in their theories?
- Which approach do you find more convincing for contemporary democratic practice, and why?
