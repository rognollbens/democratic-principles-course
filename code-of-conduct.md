# Code of Conduct for Democratic Principles Course

## Our Commitment
We are dedicated to providing a welcoming, respectful, and inclusive learning environment for all participants, regardless of background, identity, or perspective.

## Expected Behavior
- Engage in discussions with intellectual honesty and openness.
- Listen actively and consider diverse viewpoints.
- Communicate constructively, avoiding personal attacks.
- Respect confidentiality of shared experiences.
- Support fellow learners in their educational journey.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or intimidation of any kind.
- Disruptive or disrespectful communication.
- Plagiarism or academic dishonesty.
- Sharing course materials without permission.

## Reporting and Enforcement
Violations can be reported to the instructor. The instructor will address issues promptly and may take appropriate corrective actions.

## Digital Citizenship
Participants are expected to uphold ethical standards in online interactions, including proper citation of sources and respectful discourse on digital platforms.
