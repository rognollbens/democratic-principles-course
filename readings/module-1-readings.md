# Reading List – Module 1: Origins of Democratic Thought

## Required
1. **Aristotle**, *Politics* (excerpts on citizenship and constitution).
2. **John Locke**, *Second Treatise of Government* (Chapters 1‑9).
3. **Jean‑Jacques Rousseau**, *The Social Contract* (Book I).
4. **Robert A. Dahl**, *On Democracy* (Chapters 1‑4).

## Recommended
- **Plato**, *The Republic* (excerpts on democracy).
- **Alexis de Tocqueville**, *Democracy in America* (Volume I, Introduction).
- **Iris Marion Young**, *Inclusion and Democracy* (Chapter 1).

## Supplementary Resources
- PBS documentary *The Greek Revolution* (episode on democracy).
- Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy entries on "Democracy" and "Social Contract".
