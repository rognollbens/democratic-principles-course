# Reading List – Module 3: Contemporary Challenges to Democracy

## Required
1. **Steven Levitsky & Daniel Ziblatt**, *How Democracies Die* (Chapters 1‑5).
2. **Yascha Mounk**, *The People vs. Democracy* (Introduction and Part I).
3. **Cass R. Sunstein**, *#Republic* (Chapters 1‑3).
4. **Pippa Norris**, *Democratic Deficit* (Chapters 2‑4).

## Recommended
- **Timothy Snyder**, *On Tyranny* (Twenty Lessons).
- **Anne Applebaum**, *Twilight of Democracy* (Prologue).
- **Francis Fukuyama**, *Identity* (Chapter 5).

## Supplementary Resources
- Freedom House annual report *Freedom in the World*.
- V‑Dem Institute democracy reports.
- Reports from the International Institute for Democracy and Electoral Assistance.
