# Reading List – Module 2: Democratic Institutions and Processes

## Required
1. **Arend Lijphart**, *Patterns of Democracy* (Chapters 1‑3).
2. **John Stuart Mill**, *Considerations on Representative Government* (excerpts).
3. **The Federalist Papers** (Nos. 10, 51, 78).
4. **David Held**, *Models of Democracy* (Chapters 5‑7).

## Recommended
- **Guillermo O'Donnell**, *The Quality of Democracy* (Introduction).
- **Nancy L. Rosenblum**, *On the Side of the Angels* (Chapter 2).
- **Adam Przeworski**, *Democracy and the Limits of Self‑Government* (Chapter 4).

## Supplementary Resources
- Comparative Constitutions Project website.
- International IDEA electoral system design handbook.
