# Module 2: Democratic Institutions and Processes

## Overview
This module examines the structures that sustain democratic governance: constitutions, elections, separation of powers, and rule of law.

## Key Themes
- Constitutional design and amendments
- Electoral systems (majoritarian, proportional, mixed)
- Checks and balances
- Independent judiciary and free press

## Lecture Outline
1. Constitutional Frameworks: Written vs. Unwritten
2. Electoral Systems: Comparative Analysis
3. Legislative, Executive, Judicial Functions
4. Federalism and Decentralization
5. Role of Civil Society and Media

## Discussion Questions
- How do different electoral systems affect representation?
- What safeguards prevent the concentration of power?
- How do institutions adapt to changing societal needs?
