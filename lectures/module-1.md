# Module 1: Origins of Democratic Thought

## Overview
This module traces the historical development of democratic ideas from ancient Athens through the Enlightenment to modern conceptions.

## Key Themes
- Direct vs. representative democracy
- Social contract theory
- Natural rights and popular sovereignty
- The role of citizenship

## Lecture Outline
1. Athenian Democracy: Strengths and Limitations
2. Roman Republic and Mixed Government
3. Enlightenment Thinkers: Locke, Rousseau, Montesquieu
4. American and French Revolutions
5. Expansion of Suffrage and Inclusivity

## Discussion Questions
- How did ancient democracies define "the people"?
- What are the trade-offs between direct and representative democracy?
- How have conceptions of equality evolved in democratic theory?
