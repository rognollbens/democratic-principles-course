# Module 3: Contemporary Challenges to Democracy

## Overview
This module explores modern threats to democratic stability, including polarization, disinformation, economic inequality, and authoritarian resurgence.

## Key Themes
- Erosion of democratic norms
- Populism and anti‑pluralism
- Digital misinformation and social media
- Global democratic backsliding

## Lecture Outline
1. Measuring Democratic Health: Indices and Indicators
2. Polarization and Partisan Animosity
3. Disinformation and Epistemic Crisis
4. Economic Inequality and Political Influence
5. International Pressures and Hybrid Regimes

## Discussion Questions
- Can democracies self‑correct when norms are weakened?
- How can citizens discern reliable information in a digital age?
- What role do economic policies play in sustaining democracy?
